Other promotion programmes
Cantons, cities and municipalities, as well as campaigns initiated by regional electricity supply companies, also have their own energy promotion programmes.
The report entitled “Angebote der Innovationsförderung im Energiebereich” (Innovation promotion services in the energy sector) provides an overview of the main innovation support services for companies and public sector research institutions.
